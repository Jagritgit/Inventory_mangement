# 🧾 1. INVOICE (SALES) – PRICE HANDLING

## REQUIREMENT:

* Each product has a default selling price stored in Product model

---

## BEHAVIOR:

### On selecting a product:

* Automatically fill "price per item" with product's default selling price

---

### Allow manual override:

* User can increase/decrease price per item manually

---

### TOTAL CALCULATION:

* Subtotal = Quantity × Price
* Invoice total = sum of all subtotals

---

## VALIDATION:

* Quantity must be > 0
* Price must be ≥ 0

# 🧾 2. BILL (PURCHASE) – AUTO CALCULATION

## FIELDS:

* Item
* Quantity
* Cost Price
* Tax
* Total Amount Owing (₹)

---

## BEHAVIOR:

### On selecting item:

* Automatically set cost price from product's default cost price

---

### On changing quantity:

* Subtotal = Quantity × Cost Price

---

### On adding tax:

* Total Amount = Subtotal + Tax

---

## IMPORTANT:

* All calculations should update dynamically (JavaScript)
* Also validate on backend (Django)

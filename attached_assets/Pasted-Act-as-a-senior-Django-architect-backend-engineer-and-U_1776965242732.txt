# 🧾 1. SYSTEM UNDERSTANDING (VERY IMPORTANT)

Implement two clearly separated business flows:

## Invoice (Sales)

* Represents money to RECEIVE from customers
* Reduces stock
* Contributes to REVENUE (only when Paid)

## Bill (Purchase)

* Represents money to PAY to suppliers
* Increases stock
* Contributes to EXPENSES
